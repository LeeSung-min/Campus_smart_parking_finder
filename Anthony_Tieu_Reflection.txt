My contribution to the assignment was the publish/subscribe system so that 
the clients could receive asynchronous notifications when parking spots
become free to their subscribed lot id. This included the pub/sub events,
ensuring the notifications are non-blocking, having a separate TCP
connection for each event, and dealing with slow subscribers and 
back-pressure. A bug that I encountered was the race conditions
that occured when clients subscribe or unsubscribe when events
were being published. Changing the design to include RLock allowed
for the race conditions to be dealt with and avoiding the subsequent
data corruption.
